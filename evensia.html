FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How are destination weddings cheaper than traditional, at-home weddings?

The couple only has to spend on what’s needed for their own travel, accommodations, and wedding package details. Luckily, since destination weddings are typically in beautiful, exotic locales, the need for design and decor details is minimal. And since the breath-taking surroundings do the talking for you, there’s no need to splurge on extravagant elements you would use to decorate an at-home ceremony or reception space. Lastly, Evensia has exclusive deals with many all-inclusive resorts that offer pre-designed wedding packages and we also have local ground handling agents in all locations for all local assistance. The decor at destination weddings can still be customized and all for one bundled price that is usually cheaper than a banquet hall’s package. For more tips on how to save money on a destination wedding, call your Destination Wedding Specialist at Evensia today.

How do I ensure my guests can afford to attend our destination wedding?

Traditionally, the best way to help ensure that your guests can afford to attend your destination wedding is to plan in advance, so they have ample time to plan and budget accordingly. It’s also important to consider the resort, destination, and time of year you’d like to marry. For example, a wedding in Thailand or Bali is more accessible and affordable and your guests will also like the idea of attending the wedding and extended their stay. Our hotels and resorts partners in Asia are well-versed in working with budgets of all sizes. We also work out special packages and group rates for your weddings, depending on the dates and how well in advance you are ready to put down a deposit.

Why are destination weddings less stressful to plan than traditional, at-home weddings?

Choosing to have a destination wedding means putting the reigns in the hands of experts (us!) This relieves you from a lot of stress in terms of coordinating travel, accommodations for a large group and the wedding details. With someone to do it all for you, there's no reason that planning a destination wedding can't be as relaxing as laying on the beach! Destination weddings also tend to have a smaller guest list, so there's less people (and opinions) you need to keep track of. Lastly, by booking with us, there's no need to have to find separate vendors like you would for an at-home wedding. We will take care of all the co-ordination needed with the finalised hotel/resort, florist. DJ, etc. We will even accompany you to recce the destination and the hotel/resort.

How far in advance should I be planning my destination wedding?

While 6-9 months is an ideal amount of time for securing the best travel rates, we have worked with couples that have planned gorgeous weddings with only a few months (or days!) to spare. If you do have time to plan further in advance, we always advise couples to start the process sooner rather than later, particularly with large groups. We will be more than happy to assist you at every step to ensure you find your wedding planning pleasant.

Why choose a destination wedding?

An opportunity for a couple to get married in a romantic location without spending a fortune, a destination wedding removes many of the stresses of planning a regular wedding. Every wedding detail can be arranged at a resort destination to suit your taste from afar. After the wedding ceremony, the celebration can last for hours or even days, with meals and outings arranged for you and your guests.
