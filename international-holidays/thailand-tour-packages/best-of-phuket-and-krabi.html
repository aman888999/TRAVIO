OVERVIEW

One of the most popular budget holiday packages curated by our travel specialists, this Thailand trip is definitely going to impress you like none other pocket-friendly vacations. For starters, this budget trip would comprise of island-hopping to some of the most mesmerizing places, including Phra Nang Cave, Tup Island, Chicken Island, and Poda Island, also known as the four Island tour. The highlight of this Thailand holiday has to be a full-day tour to the famous yet serene Phi-Phi Island, which boasts of sun-kissed beaches, piercing-blue seawater, and spectacular rock formations. We can assure you that the adventure and beauty of these hotspots is simply unmatchable.

  • On Day 1, after your arrival at Phuket, you will be transferred to your hotel in Krabi. You can leisure around for the rest of the day.
  • On Day 2, you will proceed for a full-day four Island tour, namely, Phra Nang Cave, Tup Island, Chicken Island, and Poda Island, via longtail boat.
  • On Day 3, you will continue your tour by travelling to Phuket from Krabi.
  • On Day 4, you will leave for an adventurous Phi-Phi Island tour by big boat.  
  • On Day 5, you will set out for a half-day city tour of Phuket.
  • On Day 6, you will be transferred to the airport from Phuket to catch your flight back home.

ITINERARY

DAY 1

Arrival in Phuket; Phuket to Krabi

After you arrive at Phuket, you will be welcomed by Travio’s travel representative, who will help you transfer to your Krabi, known for its stunning natural beauty. Soon, you will check into your hotel for your overnight stay.

DAY 2

4 Island Tour by Longtail Boat

After having a delectable buffet breakfast at the hotel, you will set out for a fun and adventurous day to the four islands, namely, Phra Nang Cave, Tup Island, Chicken Island, and Poda Island. The full-day tour will be conducted by a traditional and big longtail boat and cover all the hotspots of Krabi sea.

The white-sand beaches lined up with coconut palms, the piercing-blue waters of the Krabi sea and an abundant marine life will leave you in awe of the four-island tour. Soak in the warm vibes of the splendorous islands or indulge in some recreational activities like snorkelling, surfing, sunbathing, and swimming before heading for a Thai lunch.

Once this full-day tour is over, you will return to the hotel to spend the night.

DAY 3

Krabi to Phuket

After a scrumptious buffet breakfast, you will travel to Phuket for the latter half of your trip. You will be transferred to the hotel and spend the rest of the day at leisure. You can also set out to explore the markets, cafes, restaurants, and shops near the hotel.

Overnight stay at the hotel.

DAY 4

Phi-Phi Island Tour by Big Boat

Your day will begin with a delicious buffet breakfast followed by a full-day tour (via big boat) to the incredible Phi-Phi Island. Once you reach there, do not forget to go for a swim at the stunning Pile Bay Lagoon, for it is the highlight of many budget Thailand trips. Another must-do thing at Phi-Phi Island is to visit Maya Bay and perhaps, pose for a picture or two!

You can also indulge in various recreational activities, including diving, kayaking, water boarding, snorkelling, surfing, swimming, or simply soak in the divine vibes of the Island. Once you are done with lunch, you will be taken back to your hotel for the overnight stay.

DAY 5

Half-Day Phuket City Tour

After with the buffet breakfast, you will proceed for the fun-filled half-day city tour of Phuket. The tour will include a visit to some of the major attractions, including the Big Buddha, the Buddhist Temple - Wat Chalong, Kata Viewpoint and Cape Promthep - a scenic location in Phuket to capture photographs. As a part of the best low budget tour packages, this half-day city tour offers a great value for the money!

You can spend the evening beach club hopping or exploring one of the night markets.

You will then return to the hotel to spend the night.

DAY 6

Departure from Phuket

After enjoying a succulent buffet breakfast, you will be privately transferred to the airport to catch your flight back home.

WHEN TO GO

Thailand is one of the best budget travel destinations in the world. Thus, this beautiful country invites an influx of tourists all year long. The budget international trips are thus, curated for all seasons. Even so, the peak season to visit Thailand is between November and April. The weather is warm and pleasant during these months, which means it’s the perfect time to laze around on the beach. However, people book Thailand budget honeymoon packages all through the year. Also, those who are fans of moderate weather, book their packages between the months of August and October.
