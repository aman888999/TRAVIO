OVERVIEW

Italy, home of the erstwhile Roman empire is also the birthplace of Renaissance, thence you can witness those magnificent royal architecture marvels and admire the bewitching notes of music blending with art and soak in the essence of revolutionary movements of visionaries of a bygone era. Our Italy tour packages are designed to explore iconic Italy destinations like Naples - a behemoth treasure of historical monuments, Sicily-an autonomous region, Milan- the home of fashionistas from around the world. Cover the major cities of Italy on this family-special Italy holiday packages; including a gondola ride drifting through the canals of Venice, a scenic drive through the Tuscan countryside and a visit to the beautiful Capri island and Anacapri.

  • On Day 1, you will arrive in Venice and discover the city’s canals on a gondola ride.
  • On Day 2, you will visit three remarkable islands in Venice - Murano, Burano, and Torcello.
  • On Day 3, Italy tours will take you to dreamy Florence.
  • On Day 4, drive through the Tuscan countryside and stop at famous sites along the way.
  • On Day 5 of Italy tour packages, travel to historic Naples.
  • On Day 6, visit the towns of Amalfi, Positano, and Ravello.
  • On Day 7, travel via ferry and visit Capri Island and Grotta Azzurra. Later, travel on a minibus to Anacapri.
  • On Day 8, travel to Rome and go around discovering the many monuments and landmarks in Rome
  • On Day 9, you will visit Vatican City, The Sistine Chapel and the Vatican Museum.
  • On Day 10, prepare to depart to your home country as one of the best European tours comes to an end.

ITINERARY

DAY 1

Arrive in Venice; Venice: Gondola Ride in the Venice Waterways

Welcome to Venice! On arrival, you will you have to travel to your hotel and check in.

In Venice, your dreams can finally come true with a gondola ride on the waterways of Venice just like how you saw in movies. It is truly a romantic experience as you glide on the water spending precious moments with your partner. On this relaxing ride, you will pass through Rialto Bridge and many other famous monuments which help you gain a deeper understanding of the charm and culture of Venice.

You stay overnight in Venice.

DAY 2

Venice: Murano, Burano and Torcello Half-Day Island Hopping Tour

Today you will explore the 3 most remarkable and notable islands in the Venetian lagoon. The first will be Murano, which is renowned worldwide for its glass making. The glass wares are made with superior artistry and watching these masterpieces being made is a delightful experience. The second island you will visit is Burano, which is known for its colourful and beautiful homes of the fishermen lined up against the canal. Lastly, you will visit Torcello Island which will impress you with its landscape and views. You can also visit the Byzantine Church while here.

You stay overnight in Venice

DAY 3

Venice to Florence

After a lip-smacking breakfast at the hotel, you will check out from your hotel and travel via the Eurail to Florence which takes approximately 2 hours and 10 minutes. On arriving in Florence, you will check in to your hotel and are free to spend the day exploring this beautiful city at your own leisure.

You stay overnight in Florence.

DAY 4

Florence to Tuscany; Day trip to San Gimignano, Pisa and Chianti with a Winery

Breakfast will be served at the hotel after which you will go on an incredibly scenic drive through the countryside of Tuscany. Your first stop will be Gothic Siena, known famously for its old-fashioned streets.

Stroll through the vineyards, wine cellars and olive groves of Chianti after which you can indulge in a traditional lunch between the stunning Sienese hills. During lunch, a wine tasting class will be held where you can sample different kinds of wine and olive oil and even purchase them.

After lunch, you will visit San Gimignano. Here you can stroll through the maze-like cobbled streets and squares, walking between the famous 14 towers, gorging on gelato.

Your final stop will be Pisa where you can see up close and personal The Leaning Tower of Pisa, The Cathedral, The Baptistry and The Monumental Cemetery. Your full day tour ends here, and you will head back to Florence.

You stay overnight in Florence.

DAY 5

Florence to Naples

Breakfast will be served at the hotel after which you will board the Eurail to Naples which takes approximately 3 hours. Upon arrival, you will check in to your hotel and the time has come for you to explore and enjoy Naples at your own leisure.

You stay overnight in Naples.

DAY 6

Naples: Amalfi Coast Experience - Positano, Amalfi and Ravello

After a delicious breakfast, you and a small group will visit the Amalfi coast. At the small town of Positano, you can spend an hour admiring the artistry here. You will then continue to Amalfi, driving through the mesmerizing villages of Praiano, Furore and Conca dei Marini. This will be followed by a two hour stop in the town of Amalfi, known to produce hand-made paper. After Amalfi, you will visit Ravello, where you can explore this magical land for an hour noted by travellers as spiritually awakened. You will now return to Naples after Ravello.

You stay overnight in Naples.

DAY 7

Naples: Day Trip to Capri and Blue Grotto

After a yummy breakfast, you will embark on a ferry ride towards Capri Island with your group. Arrive in Capri and take another a short boat ride to the dreamy and popular destination of ‘Grotta Azzurra’ also known as Blue Grotto. This is where sunlight streams into a cavity underwater, which shine and shimmer through the seawater creating magical reflections. You will be in complete admiration of this unique and beautiful area. Don’t forget to take photos and go exploring.

You will return to the port after which you hop on a minibus and travel through a scenic route to the quaint town of Anacapri. Located nearly 918 feet above sea level, this town offers stupendous views of Naples. Continue to eat lunch at a local restaurant after which you can use this free time to explore Capri or even go shopping. As your day ends, you return to Marina Grande and be brought back to Naples.

You stay overnight in Naples.

DAY 8

Naples to Rome; Rome: Hop-on Hop off Tour

Breakfast will be served at the hotel after which you will check out from your hotel and board the Eurail to Rome which takes approximately 1 hour and 10 minutes. Arrive in Rome and check in to your hotel.

Discover the city of Rome, the capital of Italy. It is a historically renowned, heritage city. The city is well-regarded for the priceless artwork, interesting and historical museums, ancient churches, basilicas, piazzas and more. Explore this beautiful city at your own pace on the hop on hop off double-decker bus tour and as part of this, you visit Santa Maria Maggiore, Colosseum, Circo Massimo, Piazza Venezia, Vatican City and Castel Sant'Angelo, the Trevi Fountain and more.

You stay overnight at the hotel.

DAY 9

Rome: Vatican City, Sistine Chapel and Vatican Museum Tickets (no transfers included)

After breakfast, you will visit the Vatican Museums, the Sistine Chapel and the St. Peter's Basilica in Vatican City. The ancient city, the buildings, landmarks and monuments from a bygone era is what captivate you. As part of this, you also visit the Gallery of Tapestries and the Geographical Maps.

You stay overnight stay at the hotel.

DAY 10

Departure from Rome

Enjoy a delicious breakfast buffet at the hotel and leave for the Rome Airport to catch your flight back home
