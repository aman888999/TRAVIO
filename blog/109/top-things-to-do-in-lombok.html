Top Things to Do in Lombok

by Travio Travel Specialist

LOMBOK
INDONESIA

Indonesia has gained a popular name for itself as one of Asia’s thriving beach destinations in East Asia. This has got a lot to do with the influx of tourists in major travel hubs like Jakarta and Bali. Apart from these mainstream locations, Indonesia is also home to a paradisiacal getaway that goes by the name ‘Lombok’. Home to some of the most pristine beaches and verdant landscapes, this tiny island is truly nature’s gift to mankind.

Located at just a short distance from Bali, this beautiful island is easily accessible by ferry. Just hop on to a ferry boat and unravel the wonders of this tropical getaway. If you’re wondering about how you can make the best of your trip, here’s a list of the top things to do in Lombok.

Trek up the magnificent Rinjani

Set at a staggering height of 3,726 meters above sea level, this magnanimous volcano is the second-highest in the country. Its rugged terrain and unparalleled beauty draw scores of hikers and trekking enthusiasts every year. But its main attraction awaits successful hikers at the summit. At the summit, lies the azure Lake Segara Anak whose waters glimmer calmly at the bottom of the crater. Although the trail is rather challenging and steep, the view from the top is truly rewarding.

Take a day trip to Kuta, Lombok

If your idea of a holiday includes a peaceful getaway, then a visit to Kuta, Lombok is a must in your Lombok tour package. Far from the crowds, this small island has everything for a perfect beach holiday. A lazy fishing village set along the jagged coastline, Kuta is an idyllic getaway to soak in some sun and solitude. Home to white sandy beaches and wild waves, Kuta has transformed into a happening place for beach hoppers, surfers and water sport enthusiasts. But for the rather placid traveller looking for top things to do in Lombok, there is always room for leisurely strolls and peaceful meditation sessions.

Top things to do in Lombok - Camp under the stars on Bukit Pergasingan

When in Lombok Indonesia, the top things to do in Lombok include a nightcap under the stars amidst natural bounty. Surrounded by verdant paddy fields on one side and lush forests on the other, this spot is ideal for a fun-filled outdoor camping experience. The climb up the hill is not very rigorous. So, it makes for a great alternative for travellers who want to experience the thrill of nature without having to gasp for air every other minute. Apart from camps and bonfire huddles, Bukit Pergasingan is also apt for outdoor sports like paragliding and mountain biking.

Snorkelling in Gili Islands

When you’re done exploring the land and seek to take the aqua route, a great place to explore the underwater treasures is Gili Islands. Gili Trawangan, Gili Meno, and Gili Air are three tiny islands which make up the Gili Islands and the underwater scenery and ecosystems here are beyond compare. In other words, its natural beauty is simply breathtaking. As you pass by the colourful array of coral reefs, cays and marine life, you will be transported to an altogether magical world that you will not want to head to the shore. To reach Gili Trawangan from Lombok, you’ll need to take a ferry which takes anywhere between 20 minutes to 60 minutes (about 44km) depending on the operator you choose. From Gili Trawangan, you can hop on an island-hopping boat to go to the other two Gilis for more top things to do in Lombok.

Adventure sports in Gili Trawangan

Swim with the turtles or surf amidst the intrepid wave; the choice is yours when it comes to unleashing the adventure junkie in you. If you want to get your adrenaline pumping, Gili Trawangan is one of the best places to visit near Lombok. The scenic environment makes it attractive and it’s also a platform for a plethora of activities. From diving and scuba diving to snorkelling and good old surfing, this enthralling haven has everything that makes up a sporty beach destination. Again, as mentioned earlier, Gili Trawangan is just a 60-minute ferry ride away.

Party like an animal at Senggigi

If you think the scenery in Lombok is its most exciting aspect, the nightlife will blow your mind. While the island has a rustic old charm, it does not cut corners when it comes to driving the party scene up a notch. As the island is home to many vibrant bars and clubs, the nightlife is surprisingly colourful and upbeat. If you’re not much of a socializer, the light atmosphere is always great to simply let your hair loose and shake a leg or two to the beat of some fine music.

Take a hike up to Sendang Gile and Tiu Kelep Waterfalls

Senaru is the abode of two breathtaking waterfalls in Lombok. These waterfalls are picture-perfect scenes that you cannot afford to miss during your trip. As the cascading waters gush down from an incredible height, they converge into a shallow pool creating a cloud of mist. Another feature that makes these waterfalls star attractions is their strategic position to catch a glimpse of the spectacular sunrise. Apart from this, the falls are also good spots for bathing and swimming. But it is advisable to always be on your guard lest you face any misfortunate encounter in these waters.

Paragliding in South Lombok

No activity makes you feel on top of the world like paragliding. And what better place to experience this craze than amid astounding natural beauty? The topography and unspoiled nature of Lombok, especially in its southern region, make it a wonderful hub for tourists to let go of their inhibitions and experience this one-of-a-kind joyride. As you glide over sprawling hills and lush green fields, there’s a calming sense of adventure in an altogether tranquil environment. So, take a chance and experience the unique thrill of this fascinating activity.

Catch the sunset on the west coast

No trip to Lombok is complete without having a first-hand experience of the sun hitting the horizon at dusk. As the tide rises and shoreline fades into darkness, there is nothing more satisfying than simply unwinding on the beach and admiring the coastal bliss. What makes the west coast of Lombok an ideal spot to experience this is the absolute solace and quietude surrounding it. In fact, if you are lucky, there are chances you can catch a glimpse of the sunset with an entire beach just to yourself.

Explore the old town in Mataram

Take a detour from nature escapade and explore the culture of the locals in old town Mataram. Regarded as the cultural hub of the island, the capital city is one of the main attractions of Lombok. The city’s star attractions include historic temples and ornate architectural landmarks that reflect an amalgamation of Dutch and Hindu cultures. A day’s tour around the city is sufficient to highlight the cultural background and lifestyle of the community of locals.

Lombok has remained in the shadows of its sister islands for way too long. Despite its relatively smaller size, it does not fail to attract and astound travellers from far and wide with its marvellous treasures. Only once you explore and unravel the treasures of this magical place will you realize its true worth. So, go ahead and book our Lombok tour package to experience top things to do in Lombok - something you have been missing out on all this while.
