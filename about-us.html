About Us

At Travio, we offer customized holidays that meet your aspiration and expectation. Our personalized holidays are designed with meticulous attention with the best combination of handpicked hotels and travel experiences for all kinds of travellers.

Our holidays are bespoke, led by understanding you first. Our travel specialists have planned over 10,000 trips and undertaken countless others, as much across the world as in incredible India, giving us the leverage to promise you unforgettable memories for a lifetime.

What’s With the Name Travio?

Travio is a brew of holidays and love- two words that stir-up intense feelings of happiness! The name branches from connecting the Hol of Holidays and xo of XoXo, a depiction for kisses and hugs, the most common form of expressing love. We are focused on crafting a fulfilling holiday experience for each of our customers - such that you are eager to board and reluctant to return.

Who Are We?

With a team that blends Experience, Expertise and Energy, we are not just passionate travellers; but dedicated professionals who are inclined to provide excellent customer support.

Why Book With Us?

Unmatched Destination Expertise

Our team is made of individuals who have transformed their love for travel into their professions. They have first-hand knowledge of destinations and have acquired specialization certifications from tourism boards.

Strong Local Partners

Our strong local partner network built over the years allows us to negotiate the best rates with hotels, airlines and on-ground activity providers and keep our prices competitive without compromising on quality.

Personalization to Your Satisfaction

Every holiday is tailored to your requirements and we continue tweaking until it feels right to you. We are small enough to understand your requirements yet have managed multiple groups with a maximum size of 100.

24/7 Support

We are there 24/7 to handle any unforeseen situations during your trip to ensure that your holiday runs smoothly.

Secure Holiday

Our website is secure using 128-bit SSL encryption. Online payment through our payment gateway is 100% secure and your credit card/bank account information is not shared with anyone and not stored on our storage devices.

Insured Holiday
