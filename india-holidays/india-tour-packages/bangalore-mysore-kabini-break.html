OVERVIEW

This classic itinerary combines the capital city of Bangalore, with the cultural and historic city of Mysore, home to the majestic Mysore Palace. Get a chance to spot leopards, sambhar, spotted deer at Kabini Wildlife Sanctuary, nestled on the banks of Kabini River.

Wildlife

GALLERY

ITINERARY

DAY 1

Bangalore

Arrive in Bangalore, the 'Garden City' and meet our representative and proceed to the hotel. After refreshing, proceed for a sightseeing tour to visit Lalbagh, the seat of the state legislature of Karnataka - Vidhana Soudha, the vibrant Cubbon Park, Bull Temple and ISKCON temple.

Overnight stay at the hotel.

DAY 2

Bangalore - Mysore (150 kms, 3 hrs drive)

After an early breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to Mysore. After checking in and refreshing, proceed for a full day sightseeing tour covering the 12th century Chamundeswari Temple, Brindavan Gardens spread over 60 acres of land, Sri Ranga Patna, Tipu Sultan’s Summer Palace, the opulent Mysore Palace, Mysore Zoo, Golden temple and Ranganathittu Bird Sanctuary.

Overnight at the hotel.

DAY 3

Mysore - Kabini (80 kms, 1.5 hrs drive)

After a sumptuous breakfast in the hotel, check out and drive to Kabini. Kabini offers the most splendid experience with jeep safaris and boat rides to witness wildlife at a close distance. Along with the jeep safaris, Kabini also offers jungle lodges, coracle rides, boat rides, and wildlife sightings. Leopards can be easily spotted perched of trees and at the reservoir making Kabini the easiest place to spot wildlife in India. They are also home to a good healthy population of tigers. After checking in and refreshing in the hotel, proceed to visit the major attractions - Kabini River, Kabini Dam, Banasura Sagar Dam, Nagarhole National Park, Irpu Falls and Rameswara Temple.

Overnight at the hotel/resort.

DAY 4

Kabini

After breakfast at the hotel, proceed to visit a village for getting a feel of the lifestyle of locals by interacting with them. After having lunch with the locals in the village itself, head for an Elephant Jungle safari for a taste of the natural beauty of this place.

Overnight stay at hotel/resort.

DAY 5

Departure from Bangalore

After early breakfast, check out of the hotel and drive to Bangalore for heading back home.

WHEN TO GO

October to May is the best time to indulge in wildlife experience at Kabini. Summers make the terrain look very dry and parched and the rains are quite heavy in these roundabouts.
