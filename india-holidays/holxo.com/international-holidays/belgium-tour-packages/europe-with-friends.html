OVERVIEW

Looking for a vacation with friends? The 10-day Europe With Friends package covers the best of four famous European cities - London, Amsterdam, Brussels and Paris. This tour begins in the famous city of London, where you will explore the many known sites. Next, you will head to Amsterdam, where you will go on a beer tour, cheese tour, visit the famous windmills and Dutch villages. Of course, you will go on a canal cruise too, which is a must! Before your last destination, you visit Brussels. Your last stop is Paris. With so much to do and see in Paris, you will spend a good 4 days exploring all that the city has to offer.

  • On Day 1, you will arrive in the happening city of London and explore the city on your own.
  • On Day 2, you will go on a tour of London covering its major landmarks such as the Buckingham Palace, Westminster Abbey and Big Ben.
  • On Day 3, enjoy your day in London and explore on your own.
  • On Day 4, you will travel from London to Amsterdam later, go on a Beer Tour.
  • On Day 5, discover the attractions of Amsterdam on a tour, followed by a canal cruise through the world-famous canals of Amsterdam.
  • On Day 6, you will travel from Amsterdam to Brussels where you will go on a city tour and even visit Mini Europe.
  • On Day 7, you will travel from Brussels to Paris and spend time leisurely.
  • On Day 8, you will explore the sights and sounds of Paris.
  • On Day 9, you will spend the day at leisurely in Paris.
  • On Day 10, you will depart from Paris.

ITINERARY

DAY 1

Arrival in London

Upon arrival in London, you will have to travel to your hotel on your own. The city of London is known for its history, shopping spots, architectural marvels and nightlife. Spend your day exploring all these and more at your own leisurely pace.

You will stay overnight in London.

DAY 2

London: Hop-on Hop-off Bus Tour with Madame Tussauds, Marvel 4D Cinema and Star Wars Experience

After breakfast at the hotel, you will begin your journey to discover London on a city Hop on Hop off tour led on a double-decker bus. The London tour offers sightseeing routes that cover all the main sights in central London with live commentary and another route that covers all the museums. The major sights include the change of the guards at Buckingham Palace (daily through the summer), The Houses of Parliament, Westminster Abbey, Big Ben, The London Eye, Downing Street, St. Pauls Cathedral, The Tower of London, Trafalgar Square and Piccadilly. The bus tour and route give you the opportunity to come and go as you please so that you can visit several attractions around the city. After your hop on and hop off tour, you will visit Madame Tussauds along with a Marvel 4D Cinema and Star Wars Experience.

In the evening, visit Leicester Square in the heart of London, where there’s plenty to see and do including cinemas, restaurants, clubs and bars that make London such a vibrant city both at night as well as during the day.

You will stay overnight in London.

DAY 3

London: Day at Leisure

After breakfast at the hotel, the day is free to explore the city on your own with your friends.

You will stay overnight in London.

DAY 4

London to Amsterdam; Amsterdam: Beer Tour

After breakfast in the hotel, you will travel to Amsterdam. After you check in to the hotel, you will go on a small group beer tour. The quality of Dutch beer is top-notch and you get to sample fine quality of beer along with this three hour tour that’s led by an expert.

You will stay overnight in Amsterdam.

DAY 5

Amsterdam: Hop-on Hop-off Tour & Canal Cruise

After a hearty breakfast, you will go on a hop on and hop off tour of Amsterdam. On this tour, you will get to experience the outskirts of the city and view the picturesque countryside of Holland and bask in the rich culture. You can hop on and hop off as you like.

In the evening, you will go on a canal cruise. The canals of Amsterdam are famous all over the world and this evening, you will go on a canal cruise and explore this incredible waterway.

You will stay overnight in Amsterdam.

DAY 6

Amsterdam to Brussels; Brussels: Hop-on Hop-off Tour

After breakfast, you will travel to Brussels, known for its beer and chocolates, from Amsterdam. Once you reach Brussels and check into your hotel, you will get ready to go on a hop on and hop off tour. This tour even includes entrance to Mini Europe, which is a miniature park that showcases famous buildings, sites and monuments in Europe in a miniature form.

The tour will begin with at Old City Centre where you will stroll through the streets and take in the sights and sounds. Next, you will go on to visit the Grand Palace and the well-known City Hall. And so on, the adventure through Brussels continues.

You will stay overnight in Brussels.

DAY 7

Brussels to Paris

Eat a good meal in the morning for you have a long day ahead. Today, you will travel from Brussels to Paris via train. Once you reach, you can spend the day leisurely exploring the place.

You will stay overnight in Paris.

DAY 8

Paris: City Tour

After breakfast at the hotel, you will go on a half day guided panoramic city tour of Paris which lasts for three hours. This tour lets you experience Paris from three different viewpoints, namely - a ninety minute drive in the city, a cruise down the serene Seine River and an elevator ride up to the 2nd floor of the iconic Eiffel Tower. All three viewpoints lend breathtaking views and unique perspectives of the city. Once this tour ends at the Eiffel Tower, you will go on a shopping spree.

You will stay overnight in Paris.

DAY 9

Paris: Day at Leisure

Breakfast will be served at the hotel after which you will have an option of either taking a tour of Disneyland or the Louvre Museum or you can relax and wander around the city.

You will stay overnight in Paris.

DAY 10

Departure

After breakfast at the hotel, you can spend the day leisurely before you prepare to depart to your home destination.

WHEN TO GO

The best time to visit is in April to September. France is perfect all through the year but during spring, especially during April to June, its perfect as the sun is pleasant and the warmth is very welcoming. The ideal time to travel to Brussels and Amsterdam is during the spring/summer season, between the months of April and August and weather’s very pleasant and you can spend longer time outdoors exploring the many beautiful sights. You can also visit the place during autumn which is between September-November. The best time to visit London is in the spring season when the temperature is mild and the city's parks are green and blooming. However, late spring, and summer are prime tourist season bringing in eager visitors to enjoy the breathtaking experiences London and Scotland have to offer every type of tourist.
