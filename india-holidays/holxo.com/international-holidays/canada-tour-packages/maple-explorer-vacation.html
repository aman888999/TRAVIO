OVERVIEW

While on a Maple Explorer vacation with your family, you fly into Toronto and then embark on an engaging tour around the Eastern side of Canada. This 8-day trip will take you on a city tour around Toronto, a visit to the world-famous waterfalls, Niagara Falls, after which, you travel to the capital city of Ottawa via a scenic train ride. There’s more, you still get to see Montreal and Quebec City in the far east and you return back home after an unforgettable family holiday from Montreal.

  • On Day 1, on arrival in Toronto, you are taken directly to the Niagara Falls and stay overnight there.
  • On Day 2, you explore the Niagara Falls area completely and by night you are taken back to Toronto.
  • On Day 3, you go on a city tour in and around Toronto.
  • On Day 4, you go on an interesting rail journey passing by beautiful locales to the capital city, Ottawa.
  • On Day 5, you go on a city tour in and around Ottawa, the capital city of Canada. And later a rail journey takes you to Montreal.
  • On Day 6, you go on a city tour in and around Montreal.
  • On Day 7, you go on a city tour in and around Quebec City.
  • On Day 8, your trip comes to a close, and you are taken to the Montreal airport for your onward journey back home.

ITINERARY

DAY 1

Toronto to Niagara Falls

On arrival, transfer from Toronto International Airport to a hotel in Niagara. Later check-in at the hotel and evening is spent at your leisure. Don’t miss the beauty of the illuminated Niagara Falls by night, featuring a rainbow of coloured lights reflecting the falls.

You stay overnight in a hotel in Niagara Falls.

DAY 2

Niagara Falls to Toronto

After breakfast, you check-out and keep your luggage in the lobby. Then proceed to the Table Rock Center to avail your Niagara Falls Adventure Pass. Experience the Hornblower Cruise (formerly known as Maid of the Mist) – 30 minutes’ cruise taking you close to the breathtaking mists of Niagara Falls, Journey Behind the Falls - an engraved cave behind the Niagara Falls, which opens up to the mighty Horseshoe Falls, Niagara's Fury – a 4D show featuring the moving platform beneath your feet, while you are surrounded by stunning visuals, White Water Walk – a boardwalk at the very edge of the world's wildest White water!

Later, you are taken to Toronto. You stay overnight in a Toronto hotel.

DAY 3

Toronto

After breakfast, discover what lies behind Toronto’s famous skyline on a comprehensive City Tour. Experience the sights and sounds of this cosmopolitan city, highlights include Eaton Centre, City Halls, Casa Loma, Yorkville and the University of Toronto. Pass by the Rogers Centre, the CN Tower and drive along the Harbour-front for a complete impression of the city. Later in the afternoon, there is free time to explore optional activities like Harbour Cruise, Ripley’s Aquarium and much more.

You stay overnight in a Toronto hotel.

DAY 4

Toronto to Ottawa

After breakfast, check-out and proceed to the Toronto Union Station (own arrangements) to board the VIA Rail to Ottawa. Pass through quaint little towns along Lake Ontario, the historic city of Kingston and the Thousand Islands region. On arrival proceed to the hotel (own arrangements). The rest of the day is at leisure, you can explore the charm of Canada’s Capital City.

You stay overnight in an Ottawa hotel.

DAY 5

Ottawa to Montreal

After breakfast check-out and keep the luggage in the lobby, further proceed on a Tour of Ottawa (Step-on Step-off). A cosmopolitan city with Victorian architecture and renowned museums, featuring noted collections from various artists. See the parliament buildings, embassies, universities, museums, Canadian Mint, the National Gallery of Canada, the Museum of Civilization, the residence of the Prime Minister and the National Arts Centre. Drive through scenic gardens, parklands and along the Rideau Canal, one of Ottawa's picturesque waterways. In the afternoon proceed to the Ottawa station (own arrangements) to board the VIA Rail to Montreal. On arrival, you proceed to hotel (own arrangements). Later, explore Montreal’s vibrant and dynamic culture.

You stay overnight in a Montreal hotel.

DAY 6

Montreal

After breakfast, proceed on a Sightseeing Tour of Montreal. Highlights includes, a photo-stop at the beautiful Notre Dame (entrance not included) - a Basilica renowned for its finest Gothic Revival architecture with extraordinary rich interior decoration, the residential and financial districts, visit the historic Old Montreal, get an outside view of the Olympic Complex, St. Joseph Oratory (photo-stop), a view of the campus of McGill University and a panoramic view from an observation point on top of Mount Royal. Later in the afternoon, there is free time to explore optional activities like visiting the Biodome, St. Lawrence River Jet Boating and much more.

You stay overnight in a Montreal hotel.

DAY 7

Montreal to Quebec City

After breakfast, proceed on a Full-Day Visit to Quebec City, the provincial capital and discover the historical treasures and wonderful exuberant atmosphere. A professional guide will take you on a 45 minute walking tour through Place Royale, the birthplace of French civilization in North America. Tour highlights include the Upper and Lower Towns, the Parliament Buildings, the Plains of Abraham, the Citadel, Chateau Frontenac, Place d’Armes, Quebec National Assembly, the Notre Dame Basilica and a visit to Montmorency Falls. You can admire the falls view from a cable car, suspended bridge or stairs climbing up the side of the cliff (at an additional cost).

You stay overnight in a Montreal hotel.

DAY 8

Montreal

After breakfast check-out and keep the luggage in the lobby. Spend the rest of the morning at leisure. Later, transfer to the Montreal Airport to take the connecting flight on your onward destination.

INCLUSIONS

What is included

  • 1 night accommodation at Niagara Falls with breakfast
  • 2 nights accommodation at Toronto with breakfast
  • 1 night accommodation at Ottawa with breakfast
  • 2 nights accommodation at Montreal with breakfast
  • Inter-city transfers Toronto - Niagara Falls - Toronto on sharing basis
  • Inter-city transfers Toronto - Ottawa - Montreal by VIA Rail (Economy Class - Chair Car)
  • Niagara Falls and Hornblower Cruise
  • City Tour – Toronto, Ottawa, Montreal, Quebec City with Montmorency Falls
  • Departure transfers on private basis in Montreal
  • All Sightseeing tours on sharing basis with local English speaking driver/guide

WHEN TO GO

Irrespective of Canada’s size, the weather is almost similar across the country from the west to the east. Essentially, winters are very cold in all parts of Canada and summers are warm and pleasant. So that is why the spring-summer months are the best months to visit Canada. Even the fall or autumn months - September-October is good to visit certain places. So, if you are in the Eastern part of Canada and visiting the Niagara Falls, Toronto, Quebec City, Montreal or Ottawa, the best times to visit would be from May to September.
