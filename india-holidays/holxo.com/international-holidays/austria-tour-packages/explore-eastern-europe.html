OVERVIEW

Eastern Europe is one of the top places to visit in Europe with friends. Brimming with art, modern culture, unparalleled natural wonders and a thriving night life scene, there is something for everyone in your group of friends in this rather unfamiliar region. Your trip begins in the Czech city of Prague, where you can enjoy a pub crawl and beer tasting experience. You will then proceed to explore the Austrian cities of Salzburg and Vienna. These cities boast of stunning beauty of the past that resonates through its architectural creations and lively streets. From Austria, you take the train to the Hungarian capital of Budapest where the night life scene is truly spectacular.

  • On Day 1, you arrive in the beautiful city of Prague where you get to experience the best of beer tasting and a pub crawl in the evening.
  • On Day 2, spend the day exploring Prague on a hop on hop off tour.
  • On Day 3, ride the train to Salzburg and spend the day at your own pace, exploring.
  • On Day 4, see the best attractions in Salzburg with a whistle-stop, one-hour express tour by bus.
  • On Day 5, board the train to Vienna and spend the day at your leisure, exploring.
  • On Day 6, board the hop on hop off tour bus and explore the Baroque buildings of imperial Vienna.
  • On Day 7, take the train to Budapest and spend the evening partaking in a truly authentic pub crawl experience.
  • On Day 8, explore Budapest through a Segway tour and an evening Danube river cruise.
  • On the final day, you prepare to depart after a truly exciting and adventurous journey.

ITINERARY

DAY 1

Arrival in Prague; Evening Beer Tasting Experience

Begin your extraordinary journey by arriving in the city of Prague.

With the rest of the day at your leisure, you are free to spend some time exploring the old ruins of the city. Prague is overflowing with an array of bars which offer Czech lagers such as Staropramen and Budvar as well as craft beers from their microbreweries.

In the evening, prepare for beer tasting and a pub crawl experience. Step into some of the old pubs in Prague, where you can taste various kinds of beer and some real Czech specialities. If you’re lucky enough, catch hold of the special beer-guide which will enlighten you with many interesting stories about life in Prague and its inhabitants and how Czechs set the world record in beer drinking.

You will stay overnight in Prague.

DAY 2

Prague Sightseeing Tour

After relishing a delicious breakfast, proceed to explore the city of Prague with a hop on hop off 24 hours ticket which provides a complimentary river cruise, complimentary castle tour that comes as a package with the green line and brewery discount with the yellow line.

Travellers can orient themselves with the city’s attractions in three different circuits that cover different locations. Explore famous sites such as Old Town Square, Prague Castle and Strahov Monastery. After the tour, you can step into one of the many appealing restaurants to satiate yourself with some food and drinks.

You will stay overnight in Prague.

DAY 3

Prague to Salzburg

After breakfast, transfer to the train station where you can board the train to Salzburg. Upon arrival, you can transfer to the hotel and spend the rest of the day at your leisure. The culturally packed city of Salzburg has many architectural wonders to see. From Mozart’s family home to the famous Salzburg Cathedral, there’s no dearth of treasures to explore.

You will stay overnight in Salzburg.

DAY 4

Salzburg Sightseeing Tour

After breakfast, proceed for the best sights and attractions in Salzburg with this one-hour express tour by bus. Discover the history of this well-preserved medieval city, its expansive gardens and ornate castles. Marvel at the intricacies of beautifully designed Trinity church and St. Blasius Church. For art aficionados, the Mozarteum University and the Marionette Theatre are ideal places to witness operas and ballet performances. The Mirabell Palace and its verdant landscaped gardens are also a must visit.

Post that, explore the city and its attractions with Salzburg 24 hrs card. Some attractions that are included are Mozart’s Birthplace, Catacombs and Hohensalzburg Fortress.

You stay overnight in Salzburg.

DAY 5

Salzburg to Vienna

After breakfast, check out and transfer to the station on your own to board your train to Vienna. Upon arrival, transfer to the hotel.

With the rest of the day at your leisure spend the day discovering the architectural creations and gastronomic wonders of Vienna. The baroque styled architecture reflected in the city’s start attractions is truly one-of-a-kind.

You will stay overnight in Vienna.

DAY 6

Vienna Sightseeing Tour

After breakfast, venture out to explore the city on a 24 hours hop on hop off ticket. This ticket allows you to see all the leading attractions and sites in Vienna, all in a day. During this tour, you have the opportunity to learn about the ornate architectural Hofburg Palace in the heart of the city or explore the Baroque styled buildings of the city that lay along the popular Ring street or the impressive State Opera.

You can spend the evening at your leisure strolling through the city streets at your own pace.

You will stay overnight in Vienna.

DAY 7

Vienna to Budapest; Evening Ruin Pub Crawl Experience

After breakfast, proceed to the train station to board your train to Budapest.

For a truly vibrant Budapest party extravaganza, join the Budapest pub crawl to experience what a night in Budapest is like. On the tour, you will be guided by professionals throughout a list of carefully compiled list of pubs to ensure you make the most of your experience in the city. You will get a welcome shot in each venue and later, you will be led to the favourite club of Budapest.

You will stay overnight in Budapest.

DAY 8

Budapest Segway Tour and Danube River Evening Cruise

After breakfast head to the city for an exciting Budapest Segway sightseeing tour. The tour is the best way to soak it all in in the shortest span of time. During the tour, you will drive past the State Opera House, St Stephen’s Basilica and the House of Parliament. A short distance ahead, you can marvel at the grandiose beauty of Buda Castle, Matthias Church, Fisherman’s Bastion and Margaret Island.

In the evening, if you’re in for a relaxing one hour cruise, a boat ride along the Danube River is just the icing on the cake. Watch Budapest come alive at night and catch all the architectural marvels glowing in the dark.

You will stay overnight in Budapest

DAY 9

Departure from Budapest

After breakfast, check out of your hotel and head to the airport for your departure back home.

WHEN TO GO

If you’re looking to narrow down the timeline, the best time to visit Budapest, Vienna, and Prague is the months of spring and fall. During this time the weather is pleasant and the crowds are fewer. As the countries generally have a chilly climate in winter, during the summer months these places can see a large influx of tourists.
