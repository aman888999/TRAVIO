OVERVIEW

This extravagant Singapore Malaysia tour package will take you to two exclusive destinations in South East Asia – Singapore and Malaysia. Start your trip in ‘the Lion City’. Enjoy once in a lifetime experiences like the Night Safari and Sentosa Island, which you will not get anywhere else. Spend hours together in the first ever Universal Studios of South East Asia. Begin your Malaysia holiday in Kuala Lumpur, and visit the Sunway Lagoon for a day of fun and luxury.

  • On Day 1, you arrive in Singapore, you check into the hotel and you have the day at leisure for some vibrant Singapore sightseeing.
  • On Day 2, a city tour of Singapore is arranged. Later, you will visit Sentosa Island. 
  • On Day 3 of the Singapore holiday, you go on a full-day tour of Universal Studios.
  • On Day 4, in the night, a rare Night Safari is arranged, where you can watch animals in their natural habitat at night.
  • On Day 5, you will travel from Singapore to Kuala Lumpur by coach.
  • On Day 6, you will visit the well-known Sunway Lagoon, a theme park that is included with your Singapore Malaysia tour package.
  • On Day 7, prepare to depart to your home country as your memorable Malaysia holiday comes to an end.

ITINERARY

DAY 1

Arrival at Singapore; Day at Leisure

After landing in Singapore on your Singapore Malaysia tour package; you will be taken to your hotel by one of our representatives. After checking in, you will have some time to yourself to relax or explore the city on your own. Singapore is a vibrant and diverse city with a lot to do. Do visit Clarke Quay for a fun evening.

You will stay overnight in Singapore.

DAY 2

City Tour of Singapore and Sentosa Island

On the second day of your Singapore Malaysia tour package, you will go on a half day city tour of Singapore. After breakfast, you will begin your tour, which takes you to famous landmarks in the city. You will visit Orchard Road, the Merlion Park, Fullerton Hotel, the Esplanade, the Singapore Flyer, Marina Bay Sands and Helix Bridge. You will then drive through Little India to catch a whiff of delicious Indian spices and then to Chinatown to get a glimpse of the Thian Hock Kheng Temple.

In the afternoon, you leave for Sentosa Island on a cable car ride from Mount Faber. Sentosa is a man-made island built for recreational purposes. There are plenty of things to do on the island - Tiger Sky Tower, Universal Studios Singapore, SEA Aquarium, Butterfly & Insect Kingdom, etc. You will go on a tour around the island and visit celebrity wax statues at the Madame Tussauds Wax Museum, SEA Aquarium, Butterfly & Insect Kingdom and Luge and Sky Ride. You will end the day on a high note with a special presentation of a light, sound and fire show known as the ‘Wings of Time’.

You will stay overnight in Singapore.

DAY 3

Universal Studios

Today, you will go on a tour of Universal Studios, one of the most famous theme parks with 28 attractions, rides, and shows in seven themed zones. This is a once in a lifetime experience and should not be missed! It will be an extremely exciting day especially for people travelling with family and kids this is the perfect location to have fun. This Universal Studio is the first-ever theme park in South East Asia.

You will stay overnight in Singapore.

DAY 4

Night Safari

After breakfast, you have the day at leisure in the Lion City of Singapore.

Tonight, you will go on an exciting and adventurous Night Safari, a popular tourist destination, and the world's first nocturnal zoo. You will be able to see wild animals in their natural habitat. So be prepared for some wildlife watching. Head back to the hotel after the safari.

You will stay overnight in Singapore.

DAY 5

Singapore to Kuala Lumpur

After breakfast, you will check out and head to Kuala Lumpur by coach, for your Malaysia holidays. You are free to explore the city on your own in the evening. The city has a lot to offer with mosques, temples, modern shopping malls, and high rise buildings.

You will stay overnight in Kuala Lumpur.

DAY 6

Sunway Lagoon Tour - Malaysia Holiday

Today, you will visit the well-known Sunway Lagoon, a favorite theme park of tourists on a Malaysia holiday. You depart for the tour after breakfast. Spread over 88 acres, you will find 88 different activities to do. You can also take a dip in the cool waters of Africa at Sunway Lagoon. Later, you head to the longest pedestrian suspension bridge in Malaysia with a spectacular view of the city. Experience wildlife up close with 150 species of animals from around the world. There are a lot of extreme sports and adventure sports available here. Don’t forget to visit the scream park. Lastly, enjoy the man-made surf beach for a day under the son. Head back to the hotel in the evening. It's the last day of your Malaysia holiday, so you might as well spend some time to shop for gifts for your family.

You will stay overnight in Kuala Lumpur.

DAY 7

Departure

Post breakfast, you will depart for the airport and you will board the flight to your home country.

WHEN TO GO

Both Malaysia and Singapore are year round destinations. On your Singapore Malaysia Extravaganza trip, the best time to visit would be from November to April. Both countries will have a pleasant climate which will be apt for sightseeing and taking in all the different sights.
