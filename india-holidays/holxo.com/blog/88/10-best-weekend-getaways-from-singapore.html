10 Best Weekend Getaways From Singapore

by Travio Travel Specialist

WEEKEND GETAWAYS
SINGAPORE

If you’re a Singaporean or are currently placed in the city-state with a short holiday on your mind, we’ve got just the right information for you! To offer you a break from the mundane or simply add a spark to your ongoing vacation, we have a curated a list of the best weekend getaways from Singapore.

Ideal for relaxation and a bit of adventure, each of these getaways makes for short holiday destinations from Singapore that can be easily covered in a travel jiffy!

Embark on a joyous journey with our listing on the best places to visit near Singapore, as stated below:

1. Boracay, Philippines

If you’re a water baby, travelling to Boracay is your best bet! Also known as ‘the world’s most beautiful tropical island’, the Philippine gem packs in a little something for everyone. It is best known for its white sandy beaches and pristine blue waters that together paint a beautiful picture. The island getaway near Singapore mostly boasts of enjoyable weather and is great for adventure sports such as parasailing, windsurfing, and water-skiing. Underwater enthusiasts can also try snorkelling at the island’s infamous White Beach.

Situated 3-4 hours away, Boracay is a promising weekend getaway from Singapore and can be reached via a direct flight.

2. Bali, Indonesia

When it comes to nearby tourist places to visit from Singapore, Bali is a popular choice that comes to mind. This Indonesian paradise is a cultural hub of sorts. Apart from a string of ancient temples that dot its coasts, the beautiful island is also a beloved beach destination that is flocked by thousands each year. When here, pay a visit to its volcanoes or jungles or simply laze by one of the island’s many unspoiled beaches.

While in Bali, do not miss out on the top things to do. Our 8 nights itinerary is perfect for couples looking to enjoy the best of Singapore and Bali.

Bali can be reached within 2 hours and 35 mins from Singapore via a direct flight, making it one of the most preferred weekend getaways from Singapore.

3. Cameron Highlands, Malaysia

Planning a trip with members from varying age groups can be tricky, however, a destination like Cameron Highlands always come to the rescue. A short trip out of Singapore for families, the Malaysian wonder is ideal for both nature seekers as well as adventure lovers. The place is known for its cool temperatures and is the perfect choice to beat the summer heat. Some of the best activities at the Cameron Highlands include hiking or visiting one of its infamous strawberry farms. Before leaving the place, don’t forget to gorge on some of its juicy corn!

To reach the Malaysia getaway from Singapore, Cameron Highlands, take a direct flight from Singapore to Ipoh for 1.5 hours and a 2-hour drive down will get you to your destination.

4. Hội An, Vietnam

If you are already on a Singapore vacation and wish to extend it by a few days, a visit to Hội An in Vietnam can be a great choice. Famed for its authenticity, Hội An offers an immersive Vietnamese experience in an idyllic setting. To enjoy the short getaway from Singapore, soak in the sun by one of its beaches, indulge in some retail therapy or simply explore the destination by foot. When here, you can also visit the famous Thuan Tinh Island or take an enriching tour of the Hội An Silk Village. Plus, Hội An is exceptionally well-preserved and it’s a place which through the architecture reflects both local and foreign influences, which have come together and given Hội An the tag of a UNESCO World Heritage Site. 

Another of the hugely popular weekend getaways from Singapore, Hội An can be reached within 2.5 hours via a direct flight from Singapore.

5. Siem Reap, Cambodia

If visiting a UNESCO World Heritage Site over the weekend excites you, consider Siem Reap, the heritage hub of Cambodia as Singapore weekend places to go. The city makes for an ideal weekend getaway from Singapore and is a blessing of sorts for those who are culturally inclined. Angkor Wat can be visited during the day and the other parts of the city can be enjoyed during the night. Party lovers can also stroll through the infamous ‘Pub Street’ and make merry till the wee hours of morning.

Siem Reap can be reached via a direct 2-hour flight from Singapore.

6. Bintan, Indonesia

When it comes to weekend getaways from Singapore, Bintan is a name that instantly tops the list. Owing to its proximity to the city-state, the Indonesian destination has always been a popular weekend getaway from Singapore. The island is a great distraction from the hustle of everyday life and offers an immersive mix of both the Malay and Chinese cultures. Although Bintan is most popular for golfing, it also offers several avenues for bowling, horse riding, kayaking and more. Apart from strolling by its beach, one can also partake in various eco-tours here such as the Bintan Mangrove Discovery Night tour.

For a fun-filled romantic getaway to Bintan from Singapore, check out this itinerary.

The destination for a Singapore to Indonesia day trip can be reached via a 55-minute Singapore to Indonesia ferry ride from the Tanah Merah Ferry Terminal.

7. Koh Tao, Thailand

If you’re scouting for the best romantic getaways near Singapore, be sure to list down Koh Tao in Thailand on the top of your list. Although not as popular as its buzzing counterparts of Koh Samui and Koh Pha Ngan, Koh Toa makes for ideal weekend getaways from Singapore that is yet to be fully discovered by the tourist crowds. The remote island offers breathtaking vistas of blue waters and palm trees that can seamlessly be enjoyed from a hammock by the beach.

A short getaway from Singapore for couples, Koh Tao can be reached via a 1.45-hour flight from Koh Samui and then a 2-hour ferry ride to the island.

8. Desaru, Malaysia

If you’re looking for weekend getaways from Singapore that do not burn a hole in your pocket, Desaru is your place! A mere two-and-a-half-hour ride from the city-state, Desaru is among the ultimate beach getaways from Singapore for those who enjoy a laidback life! The Malaysian paradise boasts of several sandy stretches and is known to grow some of the best organic fruits and veggies. When here, don’t miss visiting the Desaru fruit farm and the island’s bustling night markets. With fresh foods, entertainment rides, and a trail of spas and resorts, Desaru promises to be one of the best weekend breaks from Singapore.

Desaru is perfect for a short trip to Malaysia from Singapore and can be reached within 2.5 hours by road.

9. Krabi, Thailand

A list of the best weekend getaways from Singapore cannot be complete without a mention on Krabi! The island is ideal for families as well as couples and hosts a range of activities for a fun-filled weekend. Some of the popular things to do in Krabi include hiking, rock-climbing, and kayaking. The destination also offers offbeat tours such as one to the Saline hot spring in Khlong Thom as well as getaways to the limestone islands of Ko Hong and Ko Gai.

It can be reached via a 1.40-hour direct flight to Phuket and a 40 min boat ride.

10. Bangkok, Thailand

Want to eat, shop, and soak in some sun? Head on to Bangkok, one of the best weekend getaways from Singapore! The popular tourist destination is perfect for those who love to stay alive and party the night away! Bursting with colours, Bangkok is known for its vibrant vibe, its wild parties, and a fleet of rejuvenating spas and massages. Come here to shop by the street markets and simply dance away the night away in style.

Bangkok can be reached via a 2.5-hour direct flight from the city-state, making it popular with tourists looking for lively weekend getaways from Singapore.

So, what are you waiting for? Pack your bags and get ready to experience the best weekend trips from Singapore and explore more places to visit near Singapore!
