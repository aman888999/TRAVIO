Exploring Barcelona With Family

by Travio Travel Specialist

SPAIN
FAMILY FRIENDLY

Barcelona, one of the most popular cities in Spain, located in the north region is a bustling hub for many locals, tourists and others who keep the city busy with activity from the sun up to the sun down. Barcelona Spain travel will reveal how Barcelona has been home to many traditions and has a number of old buildings with traditional medieval architecture, and has been host to many different sporting events. Here are the best tourist attractions in Barcelona Spain for you:

Let us plan your Spain holiday now!

Sagrada Familia

Spain has long been a dedicated Roman Catholic nation and this church in Barcelona exhibits much of this tradition. It is a newer church that was begun in 1882 and designed by Antoni Gaudi and it is still being worked on today. Pride of Barcelona tourist places, this church is a combination of both Gothic and modern-era architecture to give it a longer lasting stability. 

           

Park Guell

Park Guell was designed by Antoni Gaudi and is know to be an architectural masterpiece. Always on every list of best tourist attractions in Barcelona Spain, this is a must do for incredible views of the city. 

         

La Ramblas

Neighborhood close to the fisherman’s wharf of Barcelona offers high street shopping and a range of cafes. La Boqueria is a market within La Ramblas and one can shop for fresh fish, cheese and fruits. There are plenty of squares with fountains for children to play around. One of the most intresting places to see in Barcelona! 

                        

Beaches and Gothic District

Soak in the sun on clear beaches of Barcelona. One can take a stroll and eat in a number of cafes along the beach. Walk on the Marina road towards north crossing the Gothic district to see a few old galleries and cathedrals.
