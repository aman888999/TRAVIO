Exotic Islands and Beach Destinations for Indian Summer Travellers

by Travio Travel Specialist

BEACH
SUMMER

Exotic islands and beach destinations have always been an attraction to travellers who are looking to just chill and relax. Even though summer may be a little warm and humid for some islands / beach destinations, they remain popular for summer breaks or summer vacations.

Some of the popular destinations and islands for a beach holiday are:

Goa

Goa in summer may be a little hot and humid, however hotels and resorts have excellent summer offers which attract travellers from across India. A lot of shacks are open, restaurants / lounges / bars / nightclubs are open, making Goa a destination for revellers and family travellers looking for a destination resort for enjoyable summer vacations.

Explore our curated Goa holidays here!

Andaman Islands

Andaman Island offers crystal clear waters, white sand beaches, getaway resorts and water-based activities. While Port Blair has a bit of history, Havelock Island and Neil Island offer remote island character and superb beaches, on par with international destinations such as Maldives and Mauritius. Andaman Island is a popular summer vacations destination, typically opted for by summer beach vacations for a 5 to 7 night ex-Chennai / Kolkata / Delhi / Mumbai package.

Explore our curated Andaman holidays here!

Maldives with Sri Lanka

Maldives is an all-season luxury getaway for travellers looking for an exotic beach getaway. Maldives combines well with plantation highland areas of Sri Lanka ((Kandy - Heritage and Lakeside) and Nuwara Eliya - Tea Plantations)) for a superb 1 week holiday.

Plan and book your Maldives and Sri Lanka holiday here!

Mauritius

A little-known fact is that when it is summer in India, it is pleasant in Mauritius, making it one of the most desired places to visit during summer. If the idea is to enjoy superb drives, beach front luxury resorts with crystal clear waters and some water-based adventure activities, along-with pleasant weather, then Mauritius is perfect. Mauritius has very little nightlife areas and is a destination for family travellers looking for a rejuvenating summer holiday.

Explore our curated Mauritius holidays here!

Bali

Bali, Indonesia is a mix of beaches and countryside. Bali offers wide ranging sightseeing / experiences, extensive nightlife areas and super beach front / countryside retreats making it a perfect standalone summer holiday destination for 5-6 nights. There are adventure / activities for family travellers, villas for honeymooners / groups and a wide range of activities for tourists such as walking trails / island excursions / heritage monuments / nature parks, making it a summer vacation destination for all age groups and traveller profiles.

Explore our curated Bali holidays here!
